  • 2021/06/18: Every way of loving and being is fair and deserves respect.

    June is the LGBTQIA+ pride month. We made this homage to the LGBTQIA persons, especially to the scientists' ones (and even more especially to the ones that are our friends). Each structure pays homage to a friend of ours, and they are themes of theses and dissertations: transsexual, lesbian, bisexual, asexual, non-binary, and pansexual. Today we celebrate the chemists' day in Brazil, so, for those, the homage is doubled. We have not made all the LGBTQIA+ flags because it would be a lot of work, but we can do the ones that are missing in the coming years.

    A collective work of: @shock.vogue @howdid_igethere @johnatan_mucelini
