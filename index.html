About

He holds a bachelor's degree in Chemistry from the Federal University of Santa Catarina (2013-2016) and a Master's degree in Physical Chemistry from the São Carlos Institute of Chemistry - University of São Paulo (2016-2018). During the undergraduate and master's degrees, he employed the density functional theory to investigated properties of interest of organic molecules and oxide systems surface, respectively. Since 2018 he is a PhD student in the São Carlos Institute of Chemistry - Universidade de São Paulo, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Juarez L. F. Da Silva. His current researches address data mining of Quantum Chemistry datasets aiming to extract insights/knowledge about the materials investigated.
