Ongoing Researches

  • I am developing a data mining framework (a python module called quandarium) that allows the user to deeply analyze the relationship between the atomic environment and some property of interest, such as the cohesive energy (related to stability) and the band bag (important optical property). An important point of this methodology is that it describes the atomic systems in terms of physical meaningful features.
